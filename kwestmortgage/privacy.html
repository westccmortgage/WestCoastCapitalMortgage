Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how K West Mortgage (“we,” “us,” “our”), a mortgage information and scenario-review website powered by Sun Coast Capital Mortgage / West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc., collects and uses information when you visit KWestMortgage.com.

Last updated: 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our scenario builder and contact forms, including your name, phone number, email, preferred contact method, and details about a potential property purchase (such as purchase price, down payment, occupancy, and property type). We also collect limited technical information automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and referring source.

Cookies & Visitor ID

We use cookies and similar technologies, along with a first-party visitor identifier, to recognize returning visitors, remember preferences, and understand how the site is used. A consent banner is presented on your first visit. Non-essential cookies, analytics, and the visitor ID are activated only after you provide consent. You may decline, and you may clear cookies through your browser at any time. Declining does not prevent you from using the core informational features of the site.

Analytics & Consent

With your consent, we may use analytics tools to measure traffic and improve the site. Analytics data is used in aggregate to understand usage patterns. We do not use analytics to make automated lending decisions.

Scenario Form Data

Information you submit through the scenario builder or contact form is used to respond to your request and to allow a licensed mortgage professional to review whether mortgage options may fit your situation. Submitting a form is not a loan application and is not a commitment to lend. We may share your information with affiliated licensed mortgage professionals at Sun Coast Capital Mortgage / West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc. and with service providers that help us operate the site, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not rent or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Information is used to respond to your inquiry, provide scenario review, comply with legal obligations, and operate and improve our services.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided, and you may opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us at info@kwestmortgages.com. Depending on your state of residence, you may have additional rights regarding your personal information; we honor applicable rights under U.S. state privacy laws.

Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described here or as required by law.

Third-Party Links

The site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to info@kwestmortgages.com or (561) 956-8866.
