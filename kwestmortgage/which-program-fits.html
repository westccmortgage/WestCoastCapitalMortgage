Why one lender may say no while another program may fit

Different lenders and programs have different guidelines. A “no” on one program does not mean every path is closed — another structure may be a better match for your Key West scenario.

Why the loan program depends on your full picture

  • Income type and documentation
  • Credit profile
  • Property type and occupancy
  • Down payment and reserves
  • Loan amount

Why a scenario review helps narrow the options

Organizing the details first makes it easier to identify which paths are worth reviewing with a licensed mortgage professional — subject to guidelines and underwriting approval.

This page is educational only and not a commitment to lend. Not every buyer will qualify for every program.
