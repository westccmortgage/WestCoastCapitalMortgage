About

Mortgage Guidance for Key West Buyers

K West Mortgage is a local mortgage information and scenario-review website focused on helping Key West and Monroe County buyers better understand their financing options before they apply.

Who We Are

Local, Educational, Scenario-First

We exist to help Key West and Florida Keys buyers slow down and understand their options before writing an offer. In a high-cost market, the difference between assuming jumbo and checking high-balance conforming first can be significant — and it deserves a thoughtful conversation, not a sales pitch.

The site is powered by licensed mortgage professionals through Sun Coast Capital Mortgage / West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc. Every scenario submitted here can be reviewed by a licensed mortgage professional.

We’re an established mortgage company working in both California and Florida — so Key West and Monroe County buyers get local, Keys-focused guidance backed by a multi-state team that reviews real-world scenarios every day.
