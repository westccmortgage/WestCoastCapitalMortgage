What matters most before you go jumbo

  1. Credit score (FICO). 740+ is already strong pricing — you don’t need 780. Below 740 your rate goes up.
  2. Down payment. 20% down removes monthly mortgage insurance (PMI). Less than 20% adds PMI.
  3. Income type. W-2 is full-doc; bank statements / 1099 are Non-QM and price higher.
  4. County line. Your loan amount vs the property county’s conforming limit sets the review path.
