Rates & Payments

Understanding Key West Mortgage Rates & Payments

Mortgage rates are not one-size-fits-all. Your rate and payment may depend on credit score, loan amount, down payment, occupancy, property type, loan program, points, market conditions, lender guidelines, insurance, taxes, HOA dues, and whether the loan is conforming, high-balance conforming, jumbo, VA, FHA, DSCR, or Non-QM.

What Moves Your Number

Factors That Can Affect Your Rate & Payment

Credit profile

Credit history and score can influence the pricing you may be offered.

Loan amount & program

Conforming, high-balance conforming, jumbo, VA, FHA, DSCR and Non-QM can price differently.

Down payment

Equity and loan-to-value affect both program eligibility and payment.

Occupancy

Primary, second home, or investment property can change guidelines and pricing.

Property type

Single-family, condo, or 2–4 unit may carry different considerations.

Points & market conditions

Discount points and broader market movement factor into rate.

Insurance & flood

Coastal Key West insurance and flood costs are part of your overall payment.

Taxes & HOA dues

Property taxes and HOA dues affect your full monthly housing cost.

Lender guidelines

Program guidelines and underwriting requirements vary by scenario.

This page is educational only. It is not a rate quote, offer, or commitment to lend. We do not quote rates online. A licensed mortgage professional can review your scenario; final rate and payment are subject to qualification, program guidelines, and underwriting approval.
