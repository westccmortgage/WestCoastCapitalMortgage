DSCR Investment Loans

Key West DSCR Investment Loans

For Florida Keys investment property, a DSCR loan reviews whether the property income may support the financing — using a debt-service-coverage ratio rather than personal income documentation. Lender DSCR requirements vary by program, property, credit, reserves, and guidelines.

How DSCR is reviewed

DSCR compares estimated rental income to the property housing cost. It is a math illustration, not approval or qualification. Investors often compare DSCR with conventional investment financing.

Who it may fit in Key West

  • Investment and rental-property buyers
  • Investors comparing DSCR vs conventional
  • Buyers using rental income in the review

What we can review

A licensed mortgage professional can review estimated rents, property type, reserves, credit, and the scenario to see whether a DSCR review path may fit — subject to lender guidelines and underwriting approval.
