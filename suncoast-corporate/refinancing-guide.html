FAQ

Refinancing questions

When does refinancing make sense?
Often when you can lower your rate or payment, shorten your term, or access needed equity — and you’ll stay past your break-even point.
When might it not make sense?
If you’ll move before reaching break-even, or if costs outweigh the benefit, refinancing may not be worth it.
What does it cost to refinance?
Closing costs typically include lender, appraisal, title, and recording fees. We’ll lay them out clearly.

We’ll run the numbers with you and explain your options clearly.
